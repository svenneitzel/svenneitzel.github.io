Sven Neitzel

Hello I am Sven Neitzel and I am a Diplom Graphic Designer from Germany. I am located in Berlin and my work is mainly focused on branding, corporate design and visual communication. Through this I worked for international clients as well as for national clients in a variety of media. My conceptional approach is the key point in all designs I do. My work is highly influenced through my work experience in the Netherlands which formed my style and design thinking in both ways.
